European Union (21 events)

🇪🇺 EU — ESMA / AMLA

What's happening: AMLA launches Q3 2026 with direct supervision over highest-risk CASPs; MiCA Level 2 RTS on custody, reserves, and operational resilience publish Q2-Q4; MiCA review consultation opens August assessing DeFi, NFTs, and CASP requirements.

Deal play: CASPs under AMLA direct supervision need KYT with 10-year lookback capability and Reactor for real-time suspicious activity detection — ask: "How will you demonstrate attribution depth to AMLA supervisors when they audit your transaction monitoring in Q3?" MiCA review consultation creates early positioning window for DeFi analytics — Data Solutions and Hexagate address the NFT classification and smart contract security gaps identified in the consultation.

🇫🇷 France — AMF

What's happening: PSAN-to-MiCA transition deadline July 1, 2026 — existing registered firms must submit full MiCA authorization applications immediately.

Deal play: PSAN holders face authorization gap requiring enhanced transaction monitoring and travel rule compliance — KYT with PSAN-to-MiCA configuration and Reactor for travel rule counterparty verification closes the compliance infrastructure deficit. Ask: "What's your July 1 compliance plan if AMF rejects your PSAN transition application?"

🇩🇪 Germany — BaFin

What's happening: BaFin actively supervising crypto custody licenses under German Banking Act with ongoing MiCA harmonization through 2026.

Deal play: Banks entering crypto custody need Alterya for banking-grade wallet security and KYT for institutional transaction surveillance — ask: "How are you demonstrating BaFin-grade custody controls when traditional banking auditors don't understand blockchain security models?"

🇳🇱 Netherlands — DNB

What's happening: DNB enhancing supervision of registered crypto firms Q2 with focus on Travel Rule implementation and AML compliance quality.

Deal play: Registered VASPs need Reactor for Travel Rule originator/beneficiary data collection and KYT for enhanced due diligence on high-risk counterparties — ask: "Can your current Travel Rule solution handle DNB's enhanced supervision intensity when they audit your VASP registration in Q3?"

🇧🇪 Belgium, Norway, Spain, Sweden

Four countries executing national MiCA implementation with local AML variations: Norway extending Money Laundering Act to broader crypto activities Q3; Spain enforcing strict advertising authorization requirements; Sweden implementing national AML measures beyond EU minimums H2. Deal play: Multi-jurisdiction CASPs need Data Solutions for regulatory intelligence across diverging national requirements — one KYT configuration won't satisfy all 27 member states.

United Kingdom (6 events)

🇬🇧 UK — FCA / BoE / HMT

What's happening: FCA consultation on final crypto authorization rules closes June 30 covering conduct, custody, and market abuse; separate stablecoin payment framework advancing Q4 2026; Law Commission bill recognizing crypto as property under English law progressing H2.

Deal play: Existing crypto businesses face firm application deadlines Q3 — KYT for conduct standards and market abuse surveillance, Alterya for custody requirements, Reactor for financial crime controls. UK-authorized firms need different compliance architecture than EU MiCA firms despite framework similarities. Ask: "Are you building for FCA authorization or dual FCA-MiCA licensing?" Stablecoin issuers need KYT payment monitoring infrastructure distinct from exchange surveillance. Tokenisation consultation (July deadline) creates early positioning window for Data Solutions institutional intelligence and OSINT beneficial ownership verification.
