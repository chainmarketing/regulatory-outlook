European Union (21 events)

🇪🇺 EU — ESMA / AMLA

What's happening: Three critical timelines converge: AMLA operational launch Q3 with direct supervision of highest-risk CASPs, multiple RTS packages on custody/stablecoin reserves/operational resilience through Q4, and MiCA review consultation closing Aug 31.

Deal play: CASPs under AMLA direct supervision need KYT for enhanced transaction monitoring and Reactor for cross-border fund flow attribution—ask: "How are you demonstrating to AMLA that your TM system can detect complex layering across 27 jurisdictions?" Privacy coin ban enforcement (Jul 2027) creates 12-month runway for exchanges to implement Reactor screening for Monero/Zcash exposure before delisting requirements hit. We've already submitted positions on AMLA group-wide AML/CFT RTS and are tracking the MiCA review consultation—expect DeFi and NFT guidance to shift compliance baselines.

🇫🇷 France — AMF

What's happening: July 1, 2026 hard deadline for PSAN providers to submit MiCA authorization applications or cease operations.

Deal play: French exchanges and custodians need KYT at authorization-grade (not registration-grade) and Alterya for enhanced custody standards—ask: "What's your MiCA application timeline, and can your current TM system demonstrate 24/7 monitoring with <30min alert response?"

🇩🇪 Germany — BaFin

What's happening: BaFin actively enforcing crypto custody licensing under Banking Act with ongoing compliance audits through 2026.

Deal play: German banks adding custody need Alterya for wallet security architecture and KYT for institutional surveillance—ask: "How are you demonstrating to BaFin that your custody solution meets both MiCA operational resilience standards AND German Banking Act requirements?"

🇳🇱 Netherlands — DNB

What's happening: DNB enhancing supervision of registered crypto firms Q2 with Travel Rule implementation enforcement.

Deal play: Dutch VASPs need KYT with full Travel Rule counterparty screening and Reactor for originator tracing—ask: "Can your system demonstrate compliant Travel Rule message formatting to DNB inspectors during on-site reviews?"

🇳🇴 Norway — Finanstilsynet

What's happening: Q3 Money Laundering Act amendments extend AML requirements to broader crypto activities with enhanced VASP supervision.

Deal play: Norwegian exchanges expanding services need KYT coverage for newly-regulated activity types—ask: "Which of your product lines fall under the new AML scope, and can you demonstrate risk-based monitoring for each?"

🇪🇸 Spain — Bank of Spain

What's happening: Bank of Spain enforcing strict advertising authorization and risk warning requirements for crypto marketing campaigns through 2026.

Deal play: Spanish fintechs marketing crypto products need Data Solutions for regulatory intelligence tracking and KYT for compliance infrastructure underpinning authorization applications—ask: "How are you demonstrating to Banco de España that advertised products have adequate AML controls?"

🇸🇪 Sweden — Finansinspektionen

What's happening: FI implementing MiCA with enhanced national AML measures beyond EU minimums H2.

Deal play: Swedish CASPs need KYT configured for both MiCA baseline AND Swedish-specific requirements—ask: "Can your compliance system handle dual-regime reporting to both ESMA and FI simultaneously?"

United Kingdom (6 events)

🇬🇧 UK — FCA / BoE / HMT

What's happening: Three parallel tracks: FCA Q3 deadline for existing firms to submit authorization applications, stablecoin payment framework finalizing Q4 as separate regime, and Law Commission bill recognizing crypto as property enabling theft/fraud legal remedies H2.

Deal play: UK exchanges need KYT at authorization-grade with market abuse detection and Reactor for custody trails—ask: "What's your authorization application timeline, and can you demonstrate conduct standards compliance across custody, systems/controls, and market surveillance?" Stablecoin issuers preparing for Q4 payment framework need KYT for fiat-peg monitoring and Data Solutions for dual-regime navigation (payment regs vs. crypto authorization gateway). We've already submitted input on the FCA's conduct standards consultation. Tokenisation call-for-input (closes Jul) creates early-mover advantage—firms responding need Data Solutions for DLT regulatory clarity and KYT for tokenized securities surveillance.
