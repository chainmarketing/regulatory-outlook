European Union (39 events)

🇪🇺 EU — EC / ESMA / AMLA

What's happening: Three simultaneous regime changes. MiCA full implementation July 2026 with over 30 RTS/ITS covering CASP compliance, market abuse, custody, and stablecoin reserves. AMLA becomes operational Q3 with direct supervision authority over highest-risk CASPs—published first Single Programming Document establishing crypto as priority risk area. EU AI Act applies August to high-risk AI systems in AML/fraud detection. MiCA review consultation (deadline Aug 31) covers DeFi, NFTs, and CASP requirements. DORA operationalisation deadline July for digital resilience. NFT classification guidance expected December—determines which NFT activities require MiCA authorisation. Privacy coin ban under AMLR takes effect July 2027—Monero and Zcash prohibited, all transactions require identity verification.

Deal play: Every CASP needs Reactor for market abuse surveillance and KYT for transaction monitoring before July. Ask: "How are you meeting MiCA's real-time suspicious transaction detection requirements?" AMLA's crypto-as-priority-risk designation means enhanced supervision—exchanges and custodians need OSINT for entity due diligence.

🇫🇷 France — AMF

What's happening: PSAN firms must transition to full MiCA authorisation by July 1, 2026. AMF issuing public warnings—no extensions, no grandfathering beyond transition period. Firms operating under PSAN registration since 2020 face complete re-authorisation with enhanced governance, AML, and operational standards.

Deal play: French exchanges and wallet providers need KYT upgraded to MiCA standards and Reactor for market surveillance. Ask: "Have you started your MiCA application? The AMF isn't granting extensions."

🇩🇪 Germany — BaFin

What's happening: BaFin actively supervising crypto custody licenses under German Banking Act with ongoing enforcement. MiCA implementation adds parallel European authorisation requirement for firms already holding German custody licenses.

Deal play: German banks offering crypto custody need Alterya for MiCA-compliant segregation and KYT for enhanced transaction monitoring. Ask: "How are you mapping your German custody license to MiCA CASP requirements?"

🇳🇱 Netherlands — DNB

What's happening: Dutch central bank shifting from registration to active enforcement phase on Travel Rule compliance and AML supervision for registered crypto firms.

Deal play: Dutch exchanges need Reactor for automated Travel Rule verification and KYT threshold monitoring. Ask: "Is your Travel Rule implementation meeting DNB's real-time verification expectations?"

🇳🇴 Norway — Finanstilsynet

What's happening: Money Laundering Act amendments (Q3 2026) extend AML requirements to broader range of crypto activities with enhanced VASP supervision.

Deal play: Norwegian VASPs need KYT for expanded AML coverage and OSINT for customer due diligence. Ask: "Are you ready for Finanstilsynet's expanded VASP supervision mandate?"

🇪🇸 Spain — Bank of Spain

What's happening: Strict advertising rules for crypto products requiring authorisation and risk warnings for all marketing campaigns—active enforcement ongoing.

Deal play: Spanish exchanges need Reactor for marketing compliance monitoring. Ask: "How are you tracking compliance across all customer-facing communications?"

🇸🇪 Sweden — FI

What's happening: Finansinspektionen implementing enhanced national AML requirements for crypto firms beyond EU minimums alongside MiCA adoption.

Deal play: Swedish firms need KYT upgraded beyond MiCA baseline and OSINT for enhanced customer screening. Ask: "Are you meeting both MiCA and Sweden's enhanced national AML standards?"

United Kingdom (16 events)

🇬🇧 UK — FCA / BoE / HMT

What's happening: FCA authorisation gateway opens September 2026—all crypto firms must hold full authorisation to operate. CP26/13 consultation (closed June) clarifies which activities require authorisation: stablecoin issuance, custody, trading platforms, dealing, arranging, and staking. HM Treasury draft SI carves UK stablecoins out of dealing/arranging perimeter ahead of payments reforms. Systemic stablecoin prudential regime (January 2027) requires BoE resolution planning and operational resilience. Law Commission property recognition bill enables legal remedies for crypto theft. ECCTA enhanced verification requirements active for company registrations. Basel standards implementation (July) affects banks with crypto exposure. FCA/BoE tokenisation call for input (July) examines DLT infrastructure for securities.

Deal play: Every crypto firm in UK needs Reactor for market surveillance, KYT for transaction monitoring, and Alterya for custody standards before September gateway. Ask: "Have you submitted your authorisation application? The FCA isn't extending the deadline." Banks serving stablecoin issuers need Data Solutions for systemic risk monitoring.
