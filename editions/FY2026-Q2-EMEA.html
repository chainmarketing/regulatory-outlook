🇪🇺 EU — ESMA / AMLA / EC

What's happening: Full MiCA implementation July 1, 2026 activates CASP authorization, market abuse rules, and custody requirements across all 27 member states—largest regulatory event in crypto history. AMLA becomes operational Q3 2026 with direct supervision authority over highest-risk crypto firms, published first programming document February 2026 identifying crypto as priority risk area. MiCA review consultation opens August 31 covering DeFi, NFTs, and environmental impact. DORA cybersecurity operationalization deadline July 2026. EU AI Act full application August 2026 including high-risk financial AI systems. Digital Identity Wallet rollout December 2026. Privacy coin ban takes effect July 2027 under new AMLR.

Deal play: Every EU CASP needs KYT for continuous transaction monitoring under MiCA Article 68, Reactor for sanctions screening and suspicious activity detection, and Alterya for custody operational resilience under DORA—ask: "How are you demonstrating real-time AML surveillance capability to national competent authorities ahead of AMLA supervision?" We submitted our position on AMLA group-wide AML/CFT RTS.
