Executive Summary

A summary of the key regulatory developments across the region this quarter

Nine Asia-Pacific markets are operationalizing compliance frameworks through September, with Australia's Travel Rule enforcement (active now) and stablecoin payment licensing consultations creating immediate procurement windows. South Korea's institutional custody framework and Philippines VASP registration deadlines represent the region's largest Q3 compliance buildouts, while India's securities classification clarity and Indonesia's exchange custody standards will reshape regulated service provider requirements through H2. Northeast and Southeast Asia account for 60% of regional activity.
