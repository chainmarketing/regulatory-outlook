Executive Summary

A summary of the key regulatory developments across the region this quarter

Nine Asia-Pacific markets are simultaneously operationalizing VASP frameworks and travel rule requirements as regulatory supervision transitions from guidance to enforcement. Australia's AUSTRAC has moved to active penalty phase while Philippines BSP manages ongoing registration deadlines and South Korea's FSC drafts institutional custody standards for traditional financial institutions. The compliance infrastructure buildout represents the region's largest transaction monitoring and custody security opportunity since initial licensing regimes launched—and Q3 deadlines mean buying windows close fast.
