Executive Summary

A summary of the key regulatory developments across the region this quarter

Nine Asia-Pacific markets are operationalising VASP licensing, custody standards, and Travel Rule enforcement between June and September 2026, with South Korea's institutional custody framework and Australia's stablecoin payment licensing creating the largest compliance procurement windows. Northeast Asia (2 events)

🇭🇰 Hong Kong — HKMA / SFC

What's happening: SFC continuing crypto ETF approvals with retail access expanding through 2026 following initial approvals—no new licensing deadline but ongoing product supervision intensifying.

Deal play: Reactor for ETF issuer due diligence and underlying asset risk assessment. Ask: "Are you screening ETF underlying holdings for sanctions exposure and counterparty risk before retail distribution?"

🇰🇷 South Korea — FSC / KoFIU

What's happening: FSC institutional custody framework for banks and securities firms expected Q4 26—traditional financial institutions preparing to offer crypto custody services under regulatory supervision for first time.

Deal play: KYT for custodial transaction monitoring and Alterya for custody wallet security. Ask: "Your custody clients will expect bank-grade AML monitoring—are you building that capability now or waiting until FSC finalizes requirements?"

Southeast Asia (3 events)

🇮🇩 Indonesia — OJK / Bappebti

What's happening: OJK implementing custody standards for crypto exchanges Q3 26 following regulatory transfer from Bappebti—exchange supervision consolidating under financial services authority with enhanced custody security requirements.

Deal play: Alterya for custody infrastructure security assessment and KYT for exchange transaction monitoring. Ask: "OJK custody standards are coming Q3—can you demonstrate institutional-grade custody security and AML controls today?"

🇵🇭 Philippines — BSP

What's happening: BSP Circular 1108 VASP registration and comprehensive AML supervision in active compliance phase through H2 26—direct BSP oversight of VASPs with ongoing registration and compliance deadlines.

Deal play: KYT for BSP-compliant AML baseline and Reactor for customer entity screening. Ask: "BSP supervision is active now—are you demonstrating continuous compliance or scrambling at each deadline?"

🇹🇭 Thailand — Thai SEC / AMLO

What's happening: Thai SEC expanding licensing categories and supervision of crypto exchanges, brokers, and dealers Q3 26 with enhanced AML requirements—licensing scope broadening beyond baseline exchange regulation.

Deal play: KYT for licensing readiness and enhanced AML monitoring. Ask: "Thai SEC is expanding licensing scope Q3—are you positioned for broker and dealer categories or just baseline exchange compliance?"

South Asia (1 event)

🇮🇳 India — SEBI / FIU-IND

What's happening: SEBI clarifying which crypto assets constitute securities and fall under SEBI jurisdiction vs. RBI through H2 26—regulatory boundary between securities regulator and central bank authority being defined.

Deal play: Reactor for entity risk assessment as regulatory classification evolves and KYT for dual-regime compliance monitoring. Ask: "SEBI classification will determine your regulatory pathway—can you demonstrate compliance readiness for either outcome?"

Oceania (3 events)

🇦🇺 Australia — AUSTRAC / ASIC

What's happening: Two parallel developments—AUSTRAC moved from Travel Rule implementation to active enforcement Q2 26, while Treasury stablecoin payment licensing consultation determines how crypto payment gateways will be licensed as PSPs under reformed payments framework (H1 26 consultation deadline).

Deal play: KYT for Travel Rule compliance and stablecoin payment flow monitoring. Ask: "AUSTRAC enforcement is active now—are you confident in your Travel Rule reporting accuracy, or discovering gaps under scrutiny?"

🇳🇿 New Zealand — FMA

deal play: KYT for pre-regime AML infrastructure and Reactor for licensing readiness screening. Ask: "H1 27 regime launch means building compliance infrastructure now—are you ahead of the curve or waiting for final rules?"
