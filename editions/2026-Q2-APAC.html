Key developments: Financial Services Commission's institutional custody framework consultation closes Q4 2026, establishing bank-grade AML/CFT requirements for traditional financial institutions providing crypto custody to institutional clients. This framework elevates compliance standards beyond existing DABA Phase 2 Travel Rule requirements, requiring 12+ institutions to demonstrate real-time wallet attribution, enhanced transaction monitoring, and institutional-grade screening capabilities before custody license approval.

Deal angle: "When FSC finalizes custody rules in Q4, can your institution demonstrate bank-grade monitoring of institutional wallets using the same attribution depth your banking regulators expect for traditional custody?"

Product fit: Reactor provides institutional wallet attribution leveraging our 13-year entity graph that custody applicants need for FSC compliance. Hong Kong — HKMA / SFC

Key developments: Securities and Futures Commission continues expanding retail crypto ETF oversight following initial approvals, with retail access creating secondary surveillance requirements for underlying VASP activities and ETF market integrity monitoring. SFC's ongoing approval process increases oversight intensity for both ETF issuers and the VASPs serving as underlying custodians.

Deal angle: "As SFC expands retail ETF access, can you demonstrate continuous monitoring of underlying VASP activities feeding your ETF with the attribution depth SFC expects from licensed securities products?"

Product fit: Data Solutions provides ETF issuers with market surveillance and underlying VASP monitoring capabilities for SFC compliance. KYT enables continuous transaction monitoring of VASP custody operations supporting ETF products, creating audit trail for securities regulators.

Deal angle: "Now that BSP moved from registration to active audits, can your VASP demonstrate continuous transaction monitoring and beneficial ownership verification that survives BSP's direct supervision reviews?"
