Northeast Asia (2 events)

🇭🇰 Hong Kong — SFC / HKMA

What's happening: SFC expanding retail crypto ETF approvals following first wave—market structure normalisation underway with issuers, market makers, and custodians requiring ongoing compliance infrastructure.

Deal play: KYT for ETF market surveillance and manipulation detection, Reactor for counterparty due diligence on authorised participants. Ask: "How are you monitoring secondary market trading for manipulation patterns that SFC expects you to detect?"

🇰🇷 South Korea — FSC / KoFIU

What's happening: FSC developing institutional custody framework (Q4 2026 launch) allowing banks and securities firms to custody crypto for institutional clients—first TradFi-crypto convergence regulation requiring AML/CFT programs for traditional financial institutions entering crypto.

Deal play: KYT for custody transaction monitoring, Data Solutions for institutional wallet attribution. Coordinate with Legal on stablecoin issuer AML/CFT requirements. Ask: "Are your custody AML controls built for crypto transaction patterns, or are you adapting traditional finance controls that won't work?"

Southeast Asia (3 events)

🇮🇩 Indonesia — OJK / Bappebti

What's happening: OJK implementing custody standards for crypto exchanges (Q3 2026) following regulatory transfer from Bappebti—exchanges must meet banking-grade custody requirements under financial services supervision.

Deal play: Alterya for custody wallet security monitoring, KYT for exchange transaction surveillance. Ask: "Are your custody wallets meeting OJK's banking-grade security expectations, or treating crypto custody like software security?"

🇵🇭 Philippines — BSP

What's happening: BSP enforcing ongoing VASP registration and AML supervision under Circular 1108 (H2 2026 deadlines)—comprehensive regime with progressive compliance milestones for transaction monitoring, Travel Rule, and entity verification.

Deal play: KYT for VASP baseline compliance, Reactor for entity risk assessment and beneficial ownership verification. Ask: "Which Circular 1108 deadline hits you next, and can you demonstrate compliance today?"

🇹🇭 Thailand — Thai SEC / AMLO

What's happening: Thai SEC expanding licensing categories for exchanges, brokers, and dealers with enhanced AML requirements (Q3 2026)—supervision intensity increasing alongside market structure formalisation.

Deal play: KYT for enhanced AML transaction monitoring, Reactor for broker and dealer entity screening. Ask: "Are you ready for Thai SEC's enhanced AML expectations, or planning to retrofit compliance after licensing?"

South Asia (1 event)

🇮🇳 India — SEBI / FIU-IND

What's happening: SEBI clarifying which crypto assets constitute securities under SEBI jurisdiction vs RBI payment/commodity authority (H2 2026)—jurisdiction boundary determines surveillance, reporting, and licensing requirements.

Deal play: Reactor for securities classification and entity verification, KYT for bifurcated surveillance depending on asset classification. Ask: "Do you know which of your listed assets fall under SEBI securities rules, or are you waiting for enforcement to find out?"

Oceania (3 events)

🇦🇺 Australia — AUSTRAC / ASIC

What's happening: Two parallel frameworks: AUSTRAC shifted from Travel Rule implementation to active enforcement (Q2 2026 onwards), while Treasury consultation on stablecoin payment licensing under reformed PSP framework (H1 2026) determines KYT requirements for crypto payment gateways.

Deal play: KYT for Travel Rule compliance and stablecoin payment monitoring, Reactor for PSP licensing applications and counterparty screening. Ask: "AUSTRAC's enforcing Travel Rule now—are you compliant, or still treating this as a 2027 problem? And are you ready for stablecoin PSP licensing requirements?"

🇳🇿 New Zealand — FMA

What's happening: FMC Act amendments for crypto creating new VASP licensing regime with AML/CFT requirements (H1 2027 implementation)—12-month lead time for compliance infrastructure buildout.

Deal play: KYT for VASP licensing baseline, Reactor for firm due diligence and ongoing entity verification. Ask: "2027 sounds far away, but FMA expects you to demonstrate compliance readiness at application—are you building now or scrambling in Q4?"
