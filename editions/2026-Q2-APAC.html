What's happening: Financial Services Authority implementing banking-grade custody standards for crypto exchanges under OJK supervision following regulatory transfer from Bappebti, with Q3 2026 implementation deadline.

Deal play: Exchanges transitioning to OJK supervision need Alterya custody security assessments and KYT platform monitoring to meet traditional financial institution standards—ask whether their current custody architecture passes banking regulator scrutiny.

Deal play: VASPs hitting BSP registration deadlines need KYT transaction monitoring baseline and Reactor entity verification—ask whether they can demonstrate AML controls to central bank standards before their registration window closes.

🇹🇭 Thailand — Thai SEC

What's happening: Thai SEC expanding licensing categories and supervision of crypto exchanges, brokers, and dealers with enhanced AML requirements in Q3 2026.

Deal play: Brokers and dealers applying for expanded licensing categories need KYT compliance infrastructure and OSINT counterparty screening—ask whether their AML controls scale across multiple license types as Thai SEC supervision intensifies.
