The framework requires bank-grade AML/CFT programs including real-time transaction monitoring, wallet attribution, and counterparty screening—representing fundamental infrastructure buildout for 12+ major Korean banks that currently lack crypto surveillance capabilities. With 180-day implementation window closing Q4 26, banks face compressed procurement cycles for institutional-grade blockchain analytics before 2027 custody services launch.

Deal angle: "When FSC finalizes custody framework in Q4, how quickly can your compliance team deploy wallet monitoring and counterparty screening infrastructure for institutional clients?"

Product fit: Reactor for institutional wallet attribution and fund flow analysis across custody accounts; KYT for real-time transaction monitoring and suspicious activity detection required under FSC AML/CFT standards; OSINT for counterparty due diligence on institutional custody clients.

SFC oversight now extends to ETF issuers' underlying VASP relationships, custody arrangements, and market surveillance programs—creating compliance requirements for both ETF issuers and the VASPs they custody with. Retail expansion increases transaction volume monitoring requirements and manipulation detection obligations across the ETF infrastructure stack.

Deal angle: "As SFC expands retail ETF access, how are you monitoring market manipulation risk and VASP custody counterparty exposure across your crypto ETF products?"

Product fit: Data Solutions for ETF issuer market surveillance and manipulation detection; KYT for underlying VASP transaction monitoring and custody flow tracking; Reactor for attribution of ETF-related wallet activity and counterparty risk assessment.

Indonesia — OJK / Bappebti

Key developments: Financial Services Authority completed regulatory transfer from Bappebti and immediately launched Q3 2026 custody standards enforcement under direct OJK supervision of crypto exchanges. 40+ Indonesian exchanges now face OJK custody requirements including wallet security, segregation standards, and transaction monitoring—enforcement begins this quarter with compliance deadlines throughout Q3. The regulatory transfer represents fundamental shift from commodity regulator (Bappebti) to financial services regulator (OJK), bringing bank-grade custody expectations to crypto exchange operations.

Deal angle: "OJK custody standards enforcement starts Q3—how are you demonstrating wallet security and transaction monitoring compliance under financial services supervision?"

Product fit: Alterya for smart contract security and wallet custody vulnerability detection meeting OJK security standards; KYT for transaction monitoring and regulatory reporting required under OJK supervision; Data Solutions for compliance automation and OJK audit trail documentation.

Registered VASPs face ongoing audit pressure through Q3-Q4 with enhanced AML supervision standards requiring continuous monitoring infrastructure upgrades.

Deal angle: "BSP compliance audits are live—can your transaction monitoring and beneficial ownership verification systems withstand Circular 1108 operational review?"
