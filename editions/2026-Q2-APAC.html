Executive Summary

A summary of the key regulatory developments across the region this quarter

Nine Asia-Pacific markets are operationalizing VASP supervision frameworks simultaneously as travel rule enforcement enters active phase across Oceania and Southeast Asia. With Australia's AUSTRAC moving from implementation to enforcement, Philippines BSP managing ongoing Circular 1108 compliance deadlines, and South Korea's FSC drafting institutional custody standards for traditional financial institutions, the region represents the largest compliance infrastructure buildout opportunity since MiCA implementation. Buyers need transaction monitoring, entity screening, and custody security tools immediately—compliance gaps close fast in Q3.
