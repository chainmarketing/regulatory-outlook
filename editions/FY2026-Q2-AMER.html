United States (38 events)

🇺🇸 USA — SEC / CFTC / FinCEN / OCC

What's happening: Five simultaneous frameworks converge: GENIUS Act implementation launches July 2026 with OCC, FinCEN, FDIC, and NCUA coordinated rulemaking for stablecoin issuer AML/CFT programs; FinCEN's BSA reform final rules expected Q3 2026 fundamentally restructuring financial institution AML programs; FinCEN VASP enhanced recordkeeping (October deadline) drops SAR thresholds to $3,000 with mandatory wallet address and beneficial ownership tracking; CFTC DeFi enforcement guidance (August) establishes when protocols constitute designated contract markets; SEC investment adviser custody rules (September) require qualified custodians and segregation for digital assets. Biggest is GENIUS Act because it creates federal stablecoin supervision for the first time — every issuer needs compliant transaction monitoring infrastructure before July.

Deal play: Stablecoin issuers need KYT programs meeting FinCEN's proposed requirements before federal supervision begins — ask: "How will you demonstrate real-time sanctions screening and suspicious activity detection to your primary federal regulator?" National banks entering stablecoin custody under OCC's proposed framework need Reactor for wallet attribution and Alterya for segregation audit trails. VASPs facing October recordkeeping deadlines need Data Solutions for $3,000 SAR threshold automation. Investment advisers need Hexagate for custody security verification before September compliance. We've already submitted responses on OCC stablecoin supervision, FDIC crypto-asset application processes, FDIC GENIUS Act implementation, and FinCEN's BSA reform — positioning Chainalysis as the compliance infrastructure partner for federal stablecoin supervision.

Canada (4 events)

🇨🇦 Canada — CSA / FINTRAC / OSFI

What's happening: Three frameworks advancing: CSA implementing differentiated value-referenced crypto asset (VRCA/stablecoin) regulatory framework with September compliance deadline for trading platforms — distinct terms and conditions from other crypto assets; FINTRAC phased Travel Rule implementation continues through Q4 2026 beyond tax reporting requirements; OSFI industry engagement on risk weightings for Group 2a crypto-assets (tokenized traditional assets) under Capital and Liquidity Treatment guidelines — whether tokenized assets can serve as collateral in cross-market hedges. Biggest is VRCA framework because it creates Canada's first stablecoin-specific regulatory regime requiring platform-level compliance infrastructure.

Deal play: Crypto trading platforms need KYT solutions differentiating VRCA transaction monitoring from other crypto assets to meet CSA's September framework — ask: "How will you demonstrate distinct compliance controls for stablecoin trading versus other digital assets?" Banks and credit unions engaging with tokenized traditional assets under OSFI guidelines need Reactor for collateral verification and Data Solutions for risk weighting calculations. FINTRAC-registered entities need Reactor for Travel Rule originator/beneficiary information by Q4 deadline.

CVM crypto securities framework comment period (June deadline) establishes when crypto assets constitute securities with offering, intermediation, and custody requirements.

Deal play: Every Brazilian VASP operating under grandfathering provisions faces October compliance deadline — ask: "What's your licensing pathway and how will you demonstrate AML/CFT program compliance to BCB before grandfathering ends?" Banks and broker-dealers entering crypto custody under CVM's proposed framework need Alterya for segregation standards and KYT for securities-grade transaction monitoring.

Argentina — CNV

What's happening: CNV VASP registration regime (General Resolution N°1025) now in effect requiring US$35,000 minimum capital for registry inclusion — small providers below threshold escape regulation. Phased AML/CFT requirements implementation continues through Q4 2026.

Deal play: Argentine VASPs meeting capital thresholds need KYT programs demonstrating CNV-compliant transaction monitoring — ask: "How will you maintain registry inclusion with ongoing AML/CFT program verification?" Platforms expanding above small provider thresholds need Reactor for entity attribution meeting registration standards.
