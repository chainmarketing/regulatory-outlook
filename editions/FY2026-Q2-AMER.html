Executive Summary

The big picture — where things stand right now

The United States is implementing the most significant AML/CFT modernization in two decades through GENIUS Act stablecoin supervision and Bank Secrecy Act reforms, while 16 simultaneous federal and state-level rulemakings create immediate compliance infrastructure demand. Canada's Travel Rule implementation and Latin American VASP registration regimes (Argentina, Chile, Colombia, Cayman Islands) extend the compliance opportunity across all Americas markets. With FinCEN, FDIC, and NCUA comment periods closing through July and state stablecoin licensing bills advancing in Delaware and beyond, the next 90 days represent the biggest regulatory implementation window since MiCA.

United States (16 events)

🇺🇸 USA — FinCEN / FDIC / NCUA

What's happening: GENIUS Act implementation—three NPRMs published May defining AML/CFT program requirements for payment stablecoin issuers under federal banking supervision; FinCEN/FDIC comment periods close June-July, NCUA closes July 17.

Deal play: Every major stablecoin issuer (Circle, Paxos, others pursuing federal supervision) needs KYT infrastructure for payment flow monitoring and Reactor for issuer-side investigations. Ask: "How are you preparing transaction monitoring infrastructure for GENIUS Act supervision?" Separately, FinCEN's BSA/AML reform NPRM (comment deadline June 9) fundamentally restructures AML program requirements—every bank and MSB will reassess transaction monitoring baselines. This is the biggest compliance infrastructure refresh since BSA inception.

🇺🇸 USA — Treasury / State regulators

What's happening: Treasury consulting on principles for state-level crypto regulatory uniformity (AML/CFT data similarity standards); Delaware introduced stablecoin licensing bill under banking framework; multiple states advancing money transmitter and stablecoin laws through 2026.

Deal play: State-chartered trust companies and money transmitters operating multi-state need OSINT for licensing intelligence and KYT for coverage across fragmented state regimes. Ask: "Which states are you licensed in, and how are you tracking evolving compliance obligations across jurisdictions?"

🇺🇸 USA — SEC / CFTC

What's happening: SEC staff statement on DeFi broker-dealer registration (comment period open); consolidated audit trail consultation for digital asset transaction reporting and surveillance standards; SAB 121 replacement guidance on crypto custody by banks/broker-dealers (Q4).

Deal play: DeFi protocols with US user exposure and broker-dealers custody crypto need Hexagate for protocol-level compliance readiness and Alterya for institutional custody security. Ask: "Are you evaluating broker-dealer registration obligations for your protocol, and what surveillance infrastructure do you have?"

🇺🇸 USA — DOJ / OCC

What's happening: DOJ National Cryptocurrency Enforcement Team expanding focus on DeFi, mixer services, sanctions evasion with international coordination; OCC reviewing digital asset custody guidance for national banks (Q3); FedNow expansion exploring crypto payment integration implications for stablecoin settlements.

Deal play: Banks exploring crypto custody under OCC review and DeFi protocols facing enforcement scrutiny need Reactor for sanctions screening attribution and KYT for real-time sanctions compliance. Ask: "How are you addressing DOJ's heightened DeFi enforcement focus and mixer service attribution?"

Deal play: Argentine exchanges and VASPs need KYT baseline transaction monitoring and Reactor for entity verification under CNV registration. Ask: "Are you registered with CNV, and what transaction monitoring infrastructure supports your AML obligations?"

Chile — CMF

What's happening: Chilean Financial Market Commission developing implementing regulations for crypto service providers under 2023 Fintech Law (Law 21.521)—licensing and operational framework expected December 2026.

Deal play: Chilean platforms operating under Fintech Law need KYT for licensing preparation and OSINT for regulatory intelligence as CMF finalizes rules. Ask: "How are you preparing for CMF's Fintech Law implementing regulations by year-end?"

Colombia — SFC

What's happening: Superintendencia Financiera finalizing comprehensive regulatory framework for crypto asset service providers—central bank crypto-asset regulation draft law moving from legal gray area to defined framework with AML/CFT requirements.

Deal play: Colombian exchanges transitioning from unregulated to comprehensive SFC oversight need KYT exchange onboarding and Reactor for Colombian entity mapping. Ask: "What's your timeline for SFC framework compliance, and do you have transaction monitoring infrastructure in place?"

Cayman Islands — CIMA

What's happening: Cayman Islands Monetary Authority implementing Virtual Asset Service Provider framework with registration and AML compliance requirements through Q4 2026.

Deal play: Cayman-domiciled funds, exchanges, and VASPs need KYT for VASP registration compliance and Reactor for beneficial ownership verification under CIMA regime. Ask: "Are you pursuing CIMA VASP registration, and what AML infrastructure supports your application?"
