Executive Summary

The big picture — where things stand right now

Asia-Pacific faces the most concentrated regulatory activation in crypto history. Fourteen jurisdictions launch simultaneous VASP licensing, institutional custody standards, and AML enforcement between June and September 2026. Japan's FIEA reclassification and Vietnam's 0.1% transaction tax create immediate compliance infrastructure demand across exchanges, banks, and traditional financial institutions entering digital asset markets.

What Changed Since Last Quarter

Looking back — what moved, resolved, or escalated last quarter

  • Japan 🏛️ JFSA launches July FIEA reclassification—crypto moves from payments to investment products with securities-grade surveillance requirements. All FSA-registered exchanges must deploy insider trading detection systems by October 2026.
  • Vietnam ⚡ 0.1% transaction tax on all crypto trades effective July 1, 2026. Law on Digital Technology Industry recognizes digital assets as property. Five-year pilot trading market via licensed VASPs (VND 10T minimum capital) creates immediate market structure demand.

  • Australia 📋 Treasury response expected Q2 on token mapping consultation. AUSTRAC shifting from implementation to active Travel Rule enforcement. Scam Prevention framework implementation projected July 2026.

Northeast Asia (20 events)

🇭🇰 Hong Kong — SFC/HKMA

What's happening: Three deadlines converging: SFC tokenized securities platform authorization (November) requires exchange-grade market surveillance; HKMA issued first stablecoin licenses Q1 2026 with 36 applications under enhanced supervision; SFC/HKMA joint circular on intermediaries' VA activities published June establishes when tokenized assets require authorization.

Deal play: Platforms conducting secondary trading of tokenized securities need KYT market surveillance integration and Alterya custody infrastructure. Ask: "When does your tokenized securities platform authorization application go to SFC?"

🇯🇵 Japan — JFSA

What's happening: July FIEA reclassification moves crypto from payments to investment products—securities-grade regulation applies across all FSA-registered exchanges. October deadline: all exchanges must deploy comprehensive insider trading detection systems including pre-listing monitoring, employee trading restrictions, and suspicious transaction reporting. JFSA also developing AI governance framework for financial services.

Deal play: Reactor pre-listing surveillance and employee wallet monitoring become mandatory. KYT transaction surveillance must meet FIEA standards. Ask: "How are you meeting October's insider trading detection requirements under FIEA?"

🇰🇷 South Korea — FSC/KoFIU

What's happening: Digital Asset Framework Act Phase 2 implementation expected H2 2026. September deadline: KoFIU mandatory real-time travel rule verification with automated rejection of non-compliant transfers. FSC developing Q4 framework for banks to provide institutional crypto custody. Enhanced market manipulation detection systems required Q3 2026 with mandatory wash trading and spoofing reporting.

Deal play: Reactor real-time counterparty verification required for September travel rule automation. Alterya institutional custody for banks entering crypto markets Q4. Ask: "How will you handle real-time travel rule verification with automated rejection in September?"

Southeast Asia (18 events)

🇸🇬 Singapore — MAS

What's happening: July enhanced enforcement phase begins—FATF travel rule compliance mandatory for DPT transfers above SGD 1,500 with originator/beneficiary information. August: enhanced supervision of systemically important stablecoins requiring full backing with monthly audits. October: enhanced technology risk management, cyber hygiene, and operational resilience standards take effect for all licensed DPT providers. We submitted our position on MAS Consultation Paper P009-2026 covering prudential treatment of crypto assets on permissionless blockchains.

Deal play: Reactor travel rule infrastructure for SGD 1,500+ threshold tracing. Hexagate operational resilience monitoring for October technology risk standards. Ask: "How are you preparing for July travel rule enforcement escalation?"

🇹🇭 Thailand — Thai SEC/AMLO

What's happening: Three things moving: Thai SEC approved crypto ETF launches (Q2 2026) under framework requiring custody, portfolio limits, and suitability standards; Q3 licensing expansion for brokers and dealers with enhanced AML requirements; September AMLO travel rule becomes mandatory for transactions above THB 50,000.

Deal play: Asset managers launching crypto ETFs need KYT portfolio monitoring and Alterya custody compliance. Exchanges need Reactor travel rule infrastructure for September. Ask: "When is your broker/dealer license application going to Thai SEC?"

🇻🇳 Vietnam — SBV/MOF

What's happening: July 1, 2026: 0.1% transaction tax on all digital asset transactions goes live. Law on Digital Technology Industry recognizes digital assets as property. Five-year pilot crypto trading market via licensed VASPs requires VND 10T minimum capital.

Deal play: Every exchange and VASP needs KYT transaction reporting infrastructure for July 1 tax compliance. Data Solutions custom reporting for tax authority integration. Ask: "How are you calculating and reporting the 0.1% transaction tax starting July 1?"

🇲🇾 Malaysia — SC Malaysia/BNM

What's happening: October 2026: Comprehensive Cyber Crime Bill introduction (RM32M budget) with National Scam Response Centre restructuring. SC Malaysia enhanced custody standards require segregation, wallet security, insurance, and third-party custodian approval for all registered exchanges.

Deal play: Alterya custody infrastructure for enhanced SC Malaysia standards. Hexagate wallet security monitoring for fraud prevention. Ask: "How are you meeting SC Malaysia's third-party custodian approval requirements?"

🇮🇩 Indonesia — OJK

Deal play: Alterya exchange custody infrastructure for OJK segregation compliance. KYT transaction monitoring under enhanced supervision. Ask: "When is your custody framework implementation deadline under OJK supervision?"

🇵🇭 Philippines — BSP

What's happening: H2 2026: BSP implementing comprehensive VASP registration and AML supervision following Circular 1108. Ongoing compliance deadlines for VASPs under BSP oversight.

Deal play: KYT AML transaction monitoring for BSP compliance. Reactor suspicious transaction reporting infrastructure. Ask: "What's your VASP registration status with BSP under Circular 1108?"
