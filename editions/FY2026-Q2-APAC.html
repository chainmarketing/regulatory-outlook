Northeast Asia (20 events)

🇯🇵 Japan — JFSA

What's happening: JFSA reclassifies crypto from Payment Services Act to FIEA effective July 2026—crypto becomes investment product with securities-grade regulation. All FSA-registered exchanges must deploy comprehensive insider trading detection systems by October 2026, including pre-listing monitoring, employee trading restrictions, and suspicious transaction reporting.

Deal play: Every registered exchange needs Reactor for employee wallet monitoring and pre-listing attribution intelligence. Ask: "How are you detecting insider trading across employee wallets before the October deadline?"

🇭🇰 Hong Kong — SFC/HKMA

What's happening: Three concurrent deadlines: SFC/HKMA joint circular on intermediaries' VA activities published June, November tokenized securities platform authorization requiring exchange-grade market surveillance, and Q4 enhanced supervision of 36 stablecoin license applications under HKMA's August 2025 regime.

Deal play: Platforms conducting secondary trading of tokenized securities need KYT market surveillance integration and Alterya custody infrastructure. Ask: "When does your tokenized securities platform authorization application require demonstrated market surveillance capability?"

🇰🇷 South Korea — FSC/KoFIU

What's happening: Digital Asset Framework Act Phase 2 implementation timeline confirmed H2 2026 with institutional custody framework expected Q4. KoFIU launches mandatory real-time travel rule verification September 2026 with automated rejection of non-compliant transfers—no threshold, no grace period. FSC enhanced market manipulation detection standards require mandatory reporting of wash trading, spoofing, and front-running by September.

Deal play: Every VASP needs Reactor for real-time counterparty verification before automated rejection begins. Banks entering crypto custody need Alterya institutional custody standards. Ask: "How are you achieving real-time travel rule verification with automated rejection capability by September?"

Southeast Asia (18 events)

🇸🇬 Singapore — MAS

What's happening: MAS transitions from Travel Rule implementation to enhanced enforcement phase July 2026—penalties begin for non-compliance with originator/beneficiary information requirements above SGD 1,500 threshold. August enhanced supervision of systemically important stablecoins with monthly audit requirements. October technology risk management and operational resilience standards take effect for all licensed DPT providers including incident reporting and business continuity. We've already submitted our position on MAS Consultation Paper P009-2026 on cryptoassets on permissionless blockchains.

Deal play: Every licensed DPT provider needs Reactor for travel rule originator tracing and KYT for threshold compliance before enforcement escalation. Ask: "How are you ensuring SGD 1,500+ transfers include complete originator information before MAS enforcement begins?"

🇹🇭 Thailand — Thai SEC/AMLO

What's happening: AMLO travel rule becomes mandatory September 2026 for all licensed digital asset operators—originator and beneficiary information required for transactions above THB 50,000. Thai SEC expanding licensing categories Q3 with enhanced AML requirements for crypto exchanges, brokers, and dealers. Asset management companies approved to launch crypto ETFs under May framework with custody requirements and portfolio limits.

Deal play: Licensed operators need Reactor travel rule infrastructure and KYT for THB 50,000+ threshold monitoring before September. Ask: "How are you collecting and sharing travel rule information for AMLO's September deadline?"

🇻🇳 Vietnam — SBV/MOF

What's happening: 0.1% transaction tax on all digital asset transactions effective July 1, 2026. Law on Digital Technology Industry (June 2025) formally recognizes digital assets as property. Five-year pilot crypto trading market via licensed VASPs requires VND 10T minimum capital—immediate market structure buildout.

Deal play: VASPs launching under pilot need KYT transaction reporting for 0.1% tax compliance and Data Solutions for historical transaction classification. Ask: "How are you calculating and reporting 0.1% transaction tax on every trade starting July 1?"

🇮🇩 Indonesia — OJK

What's happening: OJK implementing custody standards for crypto exchanges Q3 2026 following regulatory transfer from Bappebti. Enhanced supervision includes segregation requirements, wallet security standards, insurance, and third-party custodian approval.

Deal play: Exchanges transitioning to OJK supervision need Alterya custody infrastructure and KYT for segregation verification. Ask: "How are you demonstrating custody segregation and wallet security standards to OJK by Q3?"

🇲🇾 Malaysia — SC Malaysia/BNM

What's happening: Comprehensive Cyber Crime Bill being introduced 2026 with RM32M budget. SC Malaysia targeting unlicensed foreign crypto exchanges under enhanced enforcement. October deadline for registered exchanges to comply with enhanced custody standards including segregation requirements, wallet security, insurance, and third-party custodian approval.

Deal play: Registered exchanges need Alterya custody standards compliance and KYT for wallet security monitoring before October. Ask: "How are you meeting SC Malaysia's October custody standards deadline?"

🇵🇭 Philippines — BSP

What's happening: BSP implementing comprehensive VASP registration and AML supervision following Circular 1108 with ongoing compliance deadlines H2 2026. Enhanced AML/CFT obligations include suspicious transaction reporting and customer due diligence requirements under PMLA.

Deal play: VASPs completing registration need KYT for AML supervision and Reactor for suspicious transaction detection. Ask: "How are you demonstrating AML compliance capability to BSP during VASP registration?"
