United States (16 events)

🇺🇸  — FinCEN

What's happening: BSA/AML reform NPRM public comment closes June 9—fundamentally restructures financial institution compliance programs under AMLA 2020. GENIUS Act NPRM on payment stablecoin issuer AML/CFT requirements under FinCEN supervision also live H1 for Q3 finalization.

Deal play: Banks, broker-dealers, and stablecoin issuers need KYT transaction monitoring architecture review and OSINT third-party risk assessment frameworks—ask compliance teams whether their current vendor stack supports risk-based program requirements FinCEN is codifying.

🇺🇸  — DOJ / Treasury

What's happening: Department of Justice National Cryptocurrency Enforcement Team expanding focus on DeFi protocols, mixer services, and sanctions evasion with enhanced international coordination. Treasury consultation on state-level crypto regime data similarity principles for AML/CFT uniformity runs H1.

Deal play: DeFi protocols, wallet providers, and VASPs operating multi-state need Reactor mixer exposure analysis and OSINT sanctions risk screening—enforcement expansion creates attribution urgency for platforms in regulatory gray areas.

🇺🇸  — FDIC / NCUA / OCC

What's happening: Three banking regulators operationalizing GENIUS Act simultaneously—FDIC published BSA and sanctions compliance standards NPRM for supervised stablecoin issuers, NCUA issued credit union subsidiary standards, OCC reviewing digital asset custody guidance for national banks through H2.

Deal play: Banks exploring custody or stablecoin issuance need Alterya custody security assessments and KYT issuer-grade transaction monitoring—ask whether they're building to FDIC supervision standards now or scrambling at Q4 enforcement.

🇺🇸  — SEC / CFTC

What's happening: SEC consultation on consolidated audit trail requirements for digital asset transactions proposes reporting and surveillance standards. Staff statement on DeFi broker-dealer registration comment period active H1. Delaware introduces stablecoin licensing bill under banking framework for September consideration.

Deal play: Trading platforms and DeFi protocols need Data Solutions market surveillance infrastructure and KYT institutional flow monitoring—broker-dealer designation triggers surveillance obligations competitors aren't ready for.

🇺🇸  — State Registries

What's happening: Corporate Transparency Act beneficial ownership registries being established at state level—impacts crypto company ownership disclosure requirements. Multiple states advancing stablecoin and money transmitter licensing frameworks Q2-Q4 including Delaware's banking-framework bill.

Deal play: Multi-state VASPs and stablecoin projects need OSINT entity verification for beneficial ownership filings and Data Solutions state compliance mapping—ask legal teams whether they've stress-tested ownership structures against CTA disclosure thresholds.

Canada (3 events)

OSFI industry engagement on risk weightings for Group 2a tokenized traditional assets—whether recognized as collateral under Capital and Liquidity Treatment guidelines.

Deal play: Exchanges and institutional custody providers need Alterya custody standards compliance and KYT institutional-grade monitoring—ask whether their custody architecture meets CSA's enhanced standards or requires infrastructure overhaul before Q3.

🇨🇦  — FINTRAC

What's happening: FATF Travel Rule phased compliance deadlines for Canadian crypto firms extend beyond tax reporting through Q4—multi-stage rollout creates ongoing implementation urgency.

Deal play: Canadian VASPs and exchanges need KYT counterparty screening and Reactor originator/beneficiary verification—Travel Rule isn't one deadline, it's cascading requirements through year-end that most firms are underestimating.

Deal play: Argentine VASPs need KYT exchange monitoring and Reactor entity screening—ask whether compliance teams have CNV registration timeline mapped or are waiting for enforcement to clarify requirements.

Cayman Islands — CIMA
