Executive Summary

A summary of the key regulatory developments across the region this quarter

The United States drives 67% of Americas regulatory activity this quarter as FinCEN's fundamental BSA/AML reform closes comment June 9 and GENIUS Act implementation produces coordinated stablecoin rulemaking from FDIC, FinCEN, and NCUA through Q3.
