European Union (21 events)

EU — EC / ESMA / AMLA

What's happening: MiCA review consultation closes 31 Aug—determines whether DeFi protocols require CASP authorization, how NFT crypto-asset exemptions apply, and operational requirement adjustments through 2027. AMLA launches Q3 with direct supervision over 400+ highest-risk CASPs. Three additional consultations close this quarter: ESMA/EBA suitability guidelines (25 May, retroactive monitoring), EC anti-corruption strategy (6 Jul, crypto financial crime investigation harmonization), and Central Bank of Ireland tokenisation discussion paper (5 Jun, DLT infrastructure standards).

Deal play: Reactor entity risk assessment for firms facing AMLA supervision—400+ CASPs need enhanced due diligence immediately. KYT transaction monitoring establishes ongoing compliance for MiCA authorization applications. OSINT verifies management fitness and beneficial ownership for suitability assessments.

France — AMF

What's happening: PSAN-to-MiCA transition deadline 1 July—firms must submit applications or face Q4 operating prohibition. AMF providing no grace period extensions.

Deal play: KYT establishes MiCA-compliant transaction monitoring baseline for PSAN transitions. OSINT verifies beneficial ownership and management fitness requirements for authorization applications.

Germany — BaFin

What's happening: BaFin actively enforcing crypto custody licenses under German Banking Act—ongoing supervision expectations throughout 2026 with no implementation grace period.

Deal play: Alterya secures custody infrastructure against smart contract vulnerabilities while KYT monitors custodial transaction flows for suspicious activity reporting.

Netherlands — DNB

What's happening: Dutch central bank enhancing supervision of registered crypto firms with focus on AML compliance and Travel Rule implementation Q2.

Deal play: KYT Travel Rule module ensures compliant VASP-to-VASP transfers. Data Solutions provides transaction history for DNB audit requests.

Deal play: KYT transaction monitoring baseline for expanded AML scope. Reactor identifies high-risk counterparties before regulatory examinations.

Sweden — FI

What's happening: Finansinspektionen implementing enhanced national AML requirements for crypto firms beyond EU minimums H2.

Deal play: KYT establishes compliance with Swedish-specific AML thresholds. OSINT verifies Swedish UBO requirements for licensing applications.

United Kingdom (6 events)

UK — FCA / BoE / HMT

What's happening: FCA consultation on final crypto authorization rules closes June (conduct standards, systems/controls, market abuse, custody requirements). Joint FCA/BoE tokenisation call for input closes 3 July—shapes wholesale DLT infrastructure standards. Separate stablecoin payment framework operational Q4. FCA setting firm deadlines Q3 for existing businesses to submit authorization applications under financial services registration regime.

Deal play: Reactor entity due diligence for authorization applications—firms need clean counterparty history. KYT establishes transaction monitoring controls for FCA systems/controls requirements. Hexagate secures tokenised securities infrastructure for wholesale markets consultation positioning.
