What's happening: MiCA review consultation closes 31 Aug—determines whether DeFi protocols require CASP authorization, how NFT crypto-asset exemptions apply, and if operational requirements need adjustment through 2027. AMLA launches Q3 with immediate direct supervision over 400+ highest-risk CASPs.

Deal play: Reactor entity risk assessment for firms facing AMLA supervision—400+ CASPs need enhanced due diligence immediately. KYT transaction monitoring establishes ongoing compliance for MiCA authorization applications.

What's happening: PSAN-to-MiCA transition deadline 1 July—firms must submit applications or face Q4 operating prohibition. AMF providing no grace period extensions.

Deal play: KYT establishes MiCA-compliant transaction monitoring baseline for PSAN transitions. OSINT verifies beneficial ownership and management fitness requirements for authorization applications.

What's happening: BaFin actively enforcing crypto custody licenses under German Banking Act—ongoing supervision expectations throughout 2026 with no implementation grace period.

Deal play: Alterya provides custody wallet security and key management risk assessment for Banking Act compliance. KYT monitors custodied asset flows for suspicious activity reporting.

What's happening: DNB escalating supervision of registered crypto firms Q2-Q3 with enhanced AML compliance audits and Travel Rule implementation verification.

Deal play: KYT Travel Rule solution addresses DNB's real-time verification requirements. Data Solutions provides regulatory reporting infrastructure for enhanced supervision.
