European Union (21 events)

🇪🇺 EU — EC / ESMA / AMLA

What's happening: AMLA launches Q3 with direct supervision over 400+ highest-risk CASPs—no transition grace period. MiCA review consultation closes 31 Aug and determines DeFi protocol treatment, NFT exemption boundaries, and CASP requirement adjustments through 2027. DORA operationalization (July) adds cybersecurity incident reporting for crypto infrastructure providers.

Deal play: Reactor entity risk assessment for firms facing AMLA direct supervision. KYT transaction monitoring for ongoing compliance baseline. Ask: "AMLA launches Q3 with immediate authority—are you prepared for direct supervision standards, or treating this as a 2027 problem?"

🇫🇷 France — AMF

What's happening: PSAN-to-MiCA transition deadline 1 July 2026 enforced—French crypto firms must submit full authorization applications by July 1 or face operating prohibition. Q4 represents final cutoff for completing transition.

Deal play: KYT transaction monitoring for authorization application technical standards. OSINT for beneficial ownership verification. Ask: "July 1 is 26 days away—do you have transaction monitoring documentation ready for your AMF submission?"

🇩🇪 Germany — BaFin

What's happening: BaFin continues active supervision and enforcement of crypto custody licensing under German Banking Act—ongoing 2026 compliance expectations with no transition relief.

Deal play: Alterya custody security for wallet infrastructure. KYT monitoring for custody activity oversight. Ask: "BaFin expects continuous compliance, not periodic audits—how are you demonstrating real-time custody security?"

🇳🇱 Netherlands — DNB

What's happening: Dutch central bank enhancing supervision of registered crypto firms with intensified focus on AML compliance and Travel Rule implementation throughout H1-H2 2026.

Deal play: KYT Travel Rule for counterparty verification. Data Solutions for cross-border transaction screening. Ask: "DNB is escalating Travel Rule supervision—can you demonstrate real-time counterparty verification to examiners?"

🇳🇴 Norway — Finanstilsynet

What's happening: Money Laundering Act amendments for crypto extending AML requirements to broader range of crypto activities with enhanced VASP supervision—Q3 2026 implementation.

Deal play: KYT baseline AML monitoring for expanded activity scope. Ask: "Norway's AML expansion hits Q3—are you monitoring activities that weren't in scope last quarter?"

🇪🇸 Spain — Bank of Spain

What's happening: Strict advertising rules for crypto products enforced throughout 2026, requiring authorization and risk warnings for all marketing campaigns.

Deal play: Reactor entity verification for marketing compliance. Ask: "Can you prove your marketing targets only authorized entities?"

🇸🇪 Sweden — FI

What's happening: Finansinspektionen implementing MiCA with enhanced national AML measures beyond EU minimums—H2 2026 enforcement escalation expected.

Deal play: KYT transaction monitoring exceeding MiCA baseline. Ask: "Sweden requires AML standards beyond MiCA—how are you differentiating national vs. EU requirements?"

United Kingdom (6 events)

🇬🇧 UK — FCA / BoE / HMT

What's happening: Three parallel developments: FCA consultation on final crypto authorization rules closing June 2026 (conduct standards, market abuse, custody), joint FCA/BoE tokenisation call for input (3 Jul deadline) shaping wholesale markets infrastructure, and Law Commission bill recognizing crypto as property under English law (H2 passage). FCA setting firm authorization application deadlines Q3, separate stablecoin framework expected Q4.

Deal play: Reactor firm due diligence for authorization applications. KYT custody monitoring for operational standards. OSINT for beneficial ownership verification under ECCTA enhanced requirements. Ask: "FCA's authorization gateway closes Q3—do you have transaction monitoring documentation ready, or are you waiting until Q4 and risking the deadline?"
