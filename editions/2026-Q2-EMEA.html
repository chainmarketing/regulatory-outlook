European Union (21 events)

EU — EC / ESMA / AMLA

What's happening: MiCA review consultation closes 31 Aug—determines whether DeFi protocols require CASP authorization, how NFT exemptions apply, and operational adjustments through 2027. AMLA launches Q3 with direct supervision over 400+ highest-risk CASPs across EU27. Three additional consultations close: ESMA/EBA suitability guidelines (25 May), EC anti-corruption strategy (6 Jul), and Central Bank of Ireland tokenisation paper (5 Jun).

Deal play: Reactor entity risk assessment for firms facing AMLA supervision—400+ CASPs need enhanced due diligence immediately. KYT establishes ongoing transaction monitoring for MiCA authorization applications. OSINT verifies management fitness for suitability assessments.

France — AMF

What's happening: French CASPs operating under PSAN registration must transition to full MiCA authorization by 1 July 2026. AMF enforcement escalating—firms without applications face Q4 operating prohibition.

Deal play: KYT transaction monitoring baseline for PSAN-to-MiCA transition. OSINT ownership verification for enhanced authorization due diligence. Ask: "Have you submitted your MiCA application yet?"

Germany — BaFin

What's happening: BaFin actively enforcing crypto custody license requirements under German Banking Act throughout 2026—ongoing compliance expectations for all custody providers.

Deal play: Alterya smart contract security audits for custody infrastructure. KYT client transaction monitoring for custodian compliance.

Netherlands — DNB

What's happening: Dutch central bank enhancing supervision of registered crypto firms Q2 with focus on AML compliance and Travel Rule implementation.

Deal play: KYT Travel Rule compliance validation. Data Solutions API integration for real-time counterparty verification.

Belgium — RUSI Brussels

What's happening: Speaking engagement 26 Jun—opportunity for EMEA regulatory thought leadership positioning.

Deal play: Use event for relationship building with EC policymakers and AMLA officials ahead of Q3 launch.

Norway — Finanstilsynet

What's happening: Money Laundering Act amendments Q3 extending AML requirements to broader range of crypto activities and enhanced VASP supervision.

Deal play: KYT expanded activity monitoring for newly regulated services. Reactor VASP counterparty screening.

Spain — Bank of Spain

What's happening: Enforcing strict advertising rules for crypto products requiring authorization and risk warnings throughout 2026.

Deal play: Lower priority—marketing compliance doesn't drive platform deals. Monitor only.

Sweden — FI

What's happening: Finansinspektionen implementing MiCA H2 with enhanced national AML measures beyond EU minimums.

Deal play: KYT enhanced monitoring for Swedish-specific requirements. Ask: "How are you handling Sweden's stricter AML standards?"

United Kingdom (6 events)

UK — FCA / BoE / HMT

What's happening: FCA consultation on final crypto authorization rules closes June—covers conduct standards, systems and controls, market abuse, and custody. Joint FCA/BoE tokenisation consultation (3 Jul deadline) shapes wholesale securities framework. Separate stablecoin regime for fiat-backed payment instruments goes live Q4. Law Commission bill recognizing crypto as property under English law progressing H2.

Deal play: Reactor firm due diligence for authorization gateway applicants—existing crypto businesses face firm deadlines Q3 to submit applications. KYT custody and market abuse surveillance for authorization requirements. Hexagate smart contract audits for tokenisation infrastructure. Ask: "Have you mapped your authorization timeline to the FCA gateway?"
