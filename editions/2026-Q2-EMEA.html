European Union (21 events)

EU — EC / ESMA / AMLA

What's happening: AMLA launches Q3 with direct supervision over 400+ highest-risk CASPs—immediate authority, no transition grace period. MiCA review consultation closes 31 Aug and determines whether DeFi protocols require authorization, how NFT exemptions apply, and if CASP operational requirements need adjustment. DORA operationalisation July adds mandatory cybersecurity incident reporting for crypto infrastructure providers.

Deal play: Reactor entity risk assessment for firms facing AMLA direct supervision—400+ CASPs will need to demonstrate enhanced due diligence capabilities immediately. KYT transaction monitoring establishes ongoing compliance baseline. Ask: "AMLA launches Q3 with immediate supervision authority over high-risk firms—are you positioning for direct supervision standards, or treating this as a 2027 problem?"

France — AMF

What's happening: PSAN-to-MiCA transition deadline 1 July 2026 enforced—French crypto firms must submit complete MiCA authorization applications by this date or cease operations under transitional regime. Q4 represents final window for completing transition before full prohibition.

Deal play: KYT transaction monitoring for authorization application evidence. OSINT for beneficial ownership verification under enhanced MiCA standards. Ask: "Your PSAN registration expires—do you have the AML infrastructure AMF expects in a complete MiCA application by 1 July?"

Germany — BaFin

What's happening: BaFin continues active supervision and enforcement of crypto custody licensing requirements under German Banking Act—ongoing compliance expectations beyond initial authorization.

Deal play: Alterya for custody security infrastructure and KYT for transaction monitoring under Banking Act custody standards. Ask: "BaFin custody supervision is continuous—can you demonstrate operational resilience and transaction monitoring BaFin expects post-authorization?"

Netherlands — DNB

What's happening: DNB enhancing supervision of registered crypto firms Q2 with intensified focus on AML compliance and Travel Rule implementation—real-time verification becoming enforcement priority.

Deal play: KYT Travel Rule for real-time counterparty verification and Data Solutions for cross-jurisdictional compliance reporting. Ask: "DNB is escalating Travel Rule supervision—can you demonstrate real-time VASP verification, or relying on manual processes?"

Norway — Finanstilsynet

What's happening: Money Laundering Act amendments Q3 extend AML requirements to broader range of crypto activities with enhanced VASP supervision—goes beyond EU baseline MiCA requirements.

Deal play: KYT for enhanced AML transaction monitoring under expanded supervision scope. Ask: "Norway's AML amendments Q3 expand covered activities beyond MiCA baseline—does your monitoring cover the full scope Finanstilsynet will supervise?"

Spain — Bank of Spain

What's happening: Strict advertising rules for crypto products enforced—requires authorization and mandatory risk warnings for all marketing campaigns targeting Spanish consumers.

Deal play: Reactor for entity verification ensuring marketing compliance and KYT for demonstrating operational standards supporting advertising claims. Ask: "Bank of Spain requires authorization before marketing—can you prove compliance standards supporting your consumer-facing claims?"

Sweden — FI

What's happening: Finansinspektionen implementing MiCA with enhanced national AML measures H2—Sweden adding requirements beyond EU minimums for crypto firm supervision.

Deal play: KYT transaction monitoring meeting both MiCA baseline and Swedish enhanced requirements. Ask: "Sweden adds national AML measures beyond MiCA—is your monitoring calibrated for FI's enhanced expectations?"

Belgium — RUSI Brussels

What's happening: RUSI Brussels speaking engagement Jun 26 represents key policy engagement opportunity on EU AML/CFT implementation for crypto sector.

Deal play: Thought leadership positioning on AMLA supervision standards and MiCA AML integration. Opportunity to shape narrative on proportionate risk-based supervision.

Central Bank of Ireland

What's happening: Discussion Paper DP12 on DLT and tokenisation in financial services closes 5 Jun—seeking views on DLT integration, settlement infrastructure, and intermediary roles while maintaining monetary stability and consumer protection.

Deal play: Data Solutions for DLT transaction transparency and Reactor for tokenised asset entity verification. Position on how blockchain intelligence enables safe DLT adoption under Irish regulatory framework.

ESMA / EBA Joint Consultation

What's happening: Revised Suitability Assessment Guidelines for management bodies and key function holders consultation closed 25 May—incorporates MiCA requirements for CASPs, linking governance standards to AML/CFT frameworks.

Deal play: KYT and Reactor for demonstrating fitness and propriety through operational compliance capabilities. Management suitability now explicitly tied to AML/CFT effectiveness.

European Commission Anti-Corruption Strategy

What's happening: Consultation on EU Anti-Corruption Strategy closes 6 Jul—includes harmonised penalties and improved investigation measures for financial crimes involving digital assets.

Deal play: Reactor for crypto-related corruption investigation and KYT for tracing illicit financial flows. Positions blockchain intelligence as core investigative capability for financial crime authorities.

United Kingdom (6 events)

UK — FCA / BoE / HMT

What's happening: Three things moving: FCA crypto authorization final rules consultation (Jun), joint FCA/BoE tokenisation call for input (3 Jul deadline), and separate stablecoin framework Q4. Biggest is authorization rules because it sets conduct standards, systems and controls, market abuse prevention, and custody requirements for all crypto firms seeking FCA registration—firms face hard deadlines Q3 to submit applications.

Deal play: Reactor for firm due diligence in authorization applications, KYT for systems and controls evidence, Alterya for custody security standards. Joint tokenisation call creates wholesale market opportunity—Data Solutions for DLT infrastructure transparency. Ask: "FCA authorization deadline approaching—can you demonstrate the systems and controls, market abuse surveillance, and custody security FCA expects in complete applications?"
