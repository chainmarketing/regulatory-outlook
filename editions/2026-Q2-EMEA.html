Executive Summary

A summary of the key regulatory developments across the region this quarter

AMLA's Q3 operational launch creates direct EU supervision over 400+ crypto firms while MiCA review consultation (31 Aug deadline) determines DeFi and NFT treatment through 2027. European Union (21 events)

🇪🇺 EU — EC / ESMA / AMLA

What's happening: AMLA becomes operational Q3 with direct supervision over highest-risk CASPs across EU27—no transition period, immediate compliance expectations. MiCA review consultation (31 Aug deadline) determines DeFi protocol treatment, NFT exemption boundaries, and CASP requirement adjustments through 2027.

Deal play: Reactor entity risk assessment for AMLA direct supervision applications. KYT transaction monitoring for ongoing CASP compliance baseline. Ask: "Are you prepared for AMLA's direct supervision standards, or still treating MiCA as a 2027 problem?"

🇫🇷 France — AMF

What's happening: PSAN-to-MiCA transition deadline 1 July 2026 enforced—French crypto firms must submit full MiCA authorization applications or cease operations by Q4 transition completion.

Deal play: KYT for authorization baseline, OSINT for beneficial ownership verification. Ask: "Have you submitted your MiCA application, or are you risking Q4 shutdown?"

🇩🇪 Germany — BaFin

What's happening: Ongoing crypto custody license enforcement under German Banking Act—BaFin actively supervising existing licensees with elevated compliance expectations.

Deal play: Alterya for custody security infrastructure, KYT for transaction monitoring. Ask: "Is your custody operation audit-ready for BaFin's current enforcement posture?"

🇳🇱 Netherlands — DNB

What's happening: Enhanced supervision of registered crypto firms with Travel Rule enforcement acceleration—DNB tightening compliance expectations beyond registration baseline.

Deal play: KYT for Travel Rule verification, Data Solutions for cross-border flow analysis. Ask: "Can you demonstrate real-time Travel Rule compliance to DNB's enhanced standards?"

🇧🇪 Belgium — RUSI Brussels

What's happening: RUSI Brussels speaking engagement 26 June—industry visibility opportunity, no regulatory action.

Deal play: Relationship development with EU policy stakeholders. Reactor and KYT for case study content.

🇳🇴 Norway — Finanstilsynet

What's happening: Money Laundering Act amendments Q3 extend AML requirements to broader crypto activities and enhanced VASP supervision.

Deal play: KYT for expanded AML scope, Reactor for VASP supervision applications. Ask: "Are you monitoring activities that weren't VASPs under the old regime but are under Q3 amendments?"

🇪🇸 Spain — Bank of Spain

What's happening: Strict advertising rules enforcement for crypto products—authorization and risk warnings required for all marketing campaigns.

Deal play: Monitor-only; low direct compliance spend. Reactor for firm reputation due diligence if marketing violations surface.

🇸🇪 Sweden — Finansinspektionen

What's happening: MiCA implementation with enhanced national AML measures beyond EU minimums—Sweden raising compliance bar above baseline.

Deal play: KYT for elevated national AML standards, Reactor for cross-border counterparty screening. Ask: "Are you compliant with MiCA or with Sweden's higher national bar?"
