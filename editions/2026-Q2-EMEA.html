European Union (21 events)

🇪🇺  — EC / ESMA / AMLA

Three regulatory shifts converge this quarter: MiCA review consultation (Aug 31 deadline) determines DeFi and NFT treatment for next legislative cycle, AMLA operational launch (Q3) centralises high-risk CASP supervision, and DORA operationalisation (July) requires cybersecurity incident reporting across crypto infrastructure. Biggest immediate impact: AMLA direct supervision replaces national fragmentation—CASPs operating cross-border face single enforcement authority with group-wide AML/CFT standards.

Deal play: Multi-jurisdictional CASPs need KYT group-wide monitoring and OSINT entity screening before AMLA supervision kicks in—ask whether their current AML infrastructure meets centralised oversight standards or requires platform upgrade.

🇫🇷  — AMF

PSAN providers must complete MiCA authorisation transition by July 1, 2026—AMF enforcement accelerates as non-compliant firms lose operating authority. 19 registered providers face enhanced requirements including operational resilience, custody standards, and market abuse detection.

Deal play: PSAN-registered exchanges and custodians need Reactor authorisation readiness assessments and KYT compliance baseline upgrades—ask whether they've mapped PSAN registration to MiCA authorisation requirements or risk July 1 cutoff.

🇩🇪  — BaFin

BaFin actively enforces crypto custody licensing under German Banking Act with ongoing supervision expectations—no transition period, immediate compliance required for custody providers.

Deal play: German custodians and exchanges offering wallet services need Alterya custody security assessments and KYT Banking Act compliance positioning—ask whether they hold valid custody licenses or operate in regulatory gray zone.

🇳🇱  — DNB

Dutch central bank enhances supervision of registered crypto firms with focus on AML compliance and Travel Rule implementation—enforcement phase replaces implementation phase in Q2/Q3.

Deal play: Dutch-registered VASPs need KYT counterparty screening and Reactor Travel Rule compliance infrastructure—ask whether they've operationalised Travel Rule for cross-border transactions or face DNB enforcement exposure.

For tokenisation consultation respondents, Data Solutions DLT infrastructure and KYT institutional flows position for wholesale market participation.
