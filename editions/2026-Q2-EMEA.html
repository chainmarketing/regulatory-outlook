European Union (21 events)

🇪🇺 EU — EC / ESMA / AMLA

What's happening: AMLA becomes operational Q3 with direct supervision over highest-risk CASPs—no transition grace period. MiCA review consultation closes 31 Aug and determines DeFi protocol treatment, NFT exemption boundaries, and CASP requirement adjustments through 2027. Central Bank of Ireland tokenisation discussion paper (Jun 26) and EC anti-corruption strategy consultation (6 Jul) both cover crypto infrastructure standards.

Deal play: Reactor entity risk assessment for firms facing AMLA direct supervision. KYT transaction monitoring for ongoing compliance baseline. Ask: "AMLA launches Q3—are you prepared for direct supervision standards, or treating this as a 2027 problem?"

🇫🇷 France — AMF

What's happening: PSAN-to-MiCA transition deadline 1 July 2026 enforced—French crypto firms must submit full authorization applications by July or lose operating authority when Q4 transition completes.

Deal play: KYT for authorization baseline, OSINT for beneficial ownership verification in applications. Ask: "Have you submitted your MiCA application, or are you risking Q4 operating prohibition?"

🇩🇪 Germany — BaFin

What's happening: Ongoing custody license enforcement under German Banking Act continues through 2026 with no transition relief—active supervision and compliance expectations for all custodians.

Deal play: Alterya for custody security infrastructure, KYT for transaction monitoring. Ask: "How are you demonstrating custody security to BaFin when they audit your systems this quarter?"

🇳🇱 Netherlands — DNB

What's happening: Enhanced AML supervision of registered crypto firms Q2 with specific focus on Travel Rule implementation and ongoing compliance quality.

Deal play: KYT Travel Rule verification, Data Solutions for counterparty screening. Ask: "DNB is auditing Travel Rule compliance—can you demonstrate real-time verification and full coverage?"

🇳🇴 Norway — Finanstilsynet

What's happening: Money Laundering Act amendments extending AML requirements to broader crypto activities expected Q3, with enhanced VASP supervision framework.

Deal play: KYT for enhanced AML controls, Reactor for VASP counterparty screening. Ask: "Are your AML controls ready for Norway's expanded crypto supervision regime?"

🇪🇸 Spain — Bank of Spain

What's happening: Strict advertising rules for crypto products enforced throughout 2026, requiring authorization and mandatory risk warnings for all marketing campaigns.

Deal play: Reactor for entity due diligence before marketing partnerships. Ask: "Are your marketing partners properly authorized under Spanish advertising rules?"

🇸🇪 Sweden — FI

What's happening: MiCA implementation H2 with national AML measures exceeding EU minimums—enhanced requirements for crypto firms beyond baseline MiCA standards.

Deal play: KYT for enhanced national AML, Reactor for entity screening. Ask: "Sweden goes beyond MiCA minimums—are you ready for enhanced national AML requirements?"

🇧🇪 Belgium — Speaking engagement

What's happening: RUSI Brussels speaking engagement 26 June provides EU policy audience and MiCA implementation thought leadership platform.

Deal play: Post-event follow-up with attendees on AMLA readiness and MiCA authorization timelines.

United Kingdom (6 events)

🇬🇧 UK — FCA / BoE / HMT

What's happening: Three things moving: FCA crypto authorization final rules consultation (Jun 26), joint FCA/BoE tokenisation call for input (3 Jul deadline), and Q3 application deadlines for existing crypto businesses under financial services registration. Law Commission bill recognizing crypto as property enables improved legal remedies for theft and fraud. Separate stablecoin framework as payment instruments distinct from broader crypto authorization.

Deal play: Reactor for authorization applications and firm due diligence. KYT for custody and transaction monitoring standards. Ask: "FCA authorization deadline is Q3—have you submitted your application, and can you demonstrate systems and controls compliance?"
